# 3D reconstruction: Advertising [Insert Brand Name]

This project was born as part of the Computer Animation course, in Politecnico di Torino, and was created in collaboration with Marta Tasca, Giuseppe Abatiello and Marianna Mercurio .

### Project Description
The team's goal was to reconstruct a well-known Portuguese advertising campaign in a 3D environment. The work focused on visual fidelity, analyzing and replicating the cinematic language, lighting and editing rhythm of the original work, entirely within Blender.

### Workflow and Skills
For the realization, we adopted a hybrid approach to optimize production times:

* **Modeling:** Mix of optimized assets sourced from external libraries and hand-crafted custom modeling to ensure stylistic and technical consistency.
* **Animation:** All animated sequences (keyframing, cinematography and camera management) were created **entirely by hand** in Blender to obtain maximum control over the timing and dynamics of the movements.
* **Rendering & Compositing:** Evee with dedicated post-production for element integration.
